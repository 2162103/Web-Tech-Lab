About Peace and Order

Peace and Order is the way the barangay or government protect the citizen and maintain the laws that are implemented throughout the country. It is about properly dealing with any crimes that are happening to the city.


Signs are placed throughout camp 7 to manage the traffic and maintain peace to the barangay. These signs are also used to prevent innocent casualties.

Implementation

Traffic Rules

In Camp 7 traffic rules are implemented and supported throughout, such rules and regulations are given by the City of Baguio.

Security

Camp 7 enforces the baguio city rules and regulation when it comes to security. Policemen roam the night to provide a sense of security for the citizens.

Anti-Drugs

Since camp 7 follows the same rules of Baguio City, policemen also monitors the barangay to see if there are illegal drugs that are being abused.
