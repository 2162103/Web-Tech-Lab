About Camp - 7

Barangay Camp 7 is a place in Baguio wherein a large number of people are living. It’s one of the most visited place for its shops which sells “pasalubong” and good ambiance. It is located before the city proper along Kennon Road.
VISION
“A healthy barangay maintained through sustainable health and nutrition programs with the active participation, cooperation and support of the community as well as government and non-government sectors.”
MISSION
“The Camp 7 nutrition council is organized to implement health and nutrition programs geared toward the promotion of a healthy lifestyle for all barangay constituents.”

About Inclusion

What does it mean?

Inclusion means having an open mind set and giving opportunities to everyone in the community to have an equal chance of living, rights, roles, and getting rid of discrimination on race, disability, gender, medical or any other special needs

Education

Inclusion in education means that every child or student can learn and be taught equally, giving opportunity for everyone to attend school.

Social/Society

An inclusive society is a society that over-rides differences of race, gender, class, generation, and geography, and ensures inclusion, equality of opportunity as well as capability of all members of the society to determine an agreed set of social institutions that govern social interaction. (Expert Group Meeting on Promoting Social Integration, Helsinki, July 2008)
